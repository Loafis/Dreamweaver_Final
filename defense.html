The best offense is a great defense

Here at the Guild, we pride ourselves by teaching a myriad of different techniques that will keep the duck menace at bay. While some require tools to execute the technique, they are all incredibly difficult to perform but are extremely effective. One must first finely tune their body and mind to become a master of defense against these ducks. As they are an aspect of chaos, one can never be too sure of what they can come up next but be sure that it may being the most mildly unpleasant thing in your life. Luckily we have four different methods that should earn you the victory you deserve.
